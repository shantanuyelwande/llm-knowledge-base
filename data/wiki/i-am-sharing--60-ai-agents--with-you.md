# AI Agents for Business Automation

## Overview

A comprehensive collection of 60+ pre-built [[AI Agents]] and automation workflows designed to streamline business operations across [[Sales]], [[Marketing]], [[IT Operations]], [[Human Resources]], and [[Customer Support]]. These ready-to-deploy agents leverage [[Artificial Intelligence]] to eliminate manual tasks, improve consistency, and enhance team productivity.

This resource catalog documents over 60 AI-powered automation workflows that integrate with popular business tools like [[HubSpot]], [[Google Sheets]], [[Slack]], [[Discord]], and [[Gmail]]. ## Sales & Lead Management

### Lead Qualification & Scoring

#### AI Inbound Qualification
Automates the qualification of inbound leads with zero manual effort. When a prospect submits a website form, the system:
- Researches the lead's company using [[Perplexity AI]]
- Scores and qualifies the lead with [[Claude AI]]
- Filters out spam automatically
- Sends personalized email responses via [[Gmail]]
- Logs all details in [[Google Sheets]]
- Notifies the team via [[Slack]] or [[Discord]]

#### Lead Qualifier (Fillout Integration)
Activates when a form is submitted. The workflow:
1. Uses [[Perplexity AI]] to research the company
2. Rates the lead from 1-10 using AI scoring
3. Gathers deeper insights if score exceeds 4
4. Automatically adds qualified leads to [[HubSpot]]

#### VIP Lead Alert
Instantly alerts teams when high-value leads contact the company:
- Extracts sender details and company information
- Uses [[Perplexity AI]] to research company background (size, revenue, clients)
- Scores leads 1-10 using AI analysis
- Sends VIP alerts to [[Slack]] for leads scoring 7+
- Routes lower-scoring leads to [[CRM]] for alternative handling

#### Lead Appointment Qualification
Ensures consistent and fair client assessment by:
- Eliminating reviewer bias in manual qualification
- Applying standardized evaluation criteria
- Maintaining consistency across all client assessments
- Reducing decision variability

### Lead Nurturing & Outreach

#### Lead Nurturing
Automates personalized follow-up communications:
- Sends helpful articles, videos, and tips instead of generic check-ins
- Identifies optimal timing for customer outreach
- Enables sales teams to focus on genuine connections
- Maximizes conversion results

#### Automated Upselling
Identifies the perfect moment to present relevant offers to prospects, allowing sales representatives to focus on building authentic customer relationships while maximizing revenue opportunities.

#### Automatic Company Information Scraping
Collects and organizes detailed company data before sales calls:
- Instantly gathers prospect information
- Flags pain points and relevant trends
- Enables sales reps to deliver customized value propositions
- Allows teams to focus on selling rather than research

#### Client Inquiry Reply
Automates quick, high-quality responses to customer inquiries:
- Analyzes incoming emails to understand customer needs
- Drafts professional replies using AI
- Sends drafts to team members via [[Slack]] for approval
- Ensures fast, personalized responses that keep leads warm

#### Lead Gen
Converts [[Slack]] requests into targeted lead searches:
- Qualifies prospects using AI analysis
- Drafts personalized outreach emails when email addresses are available
- Logs qualified leads in [[ActivePieces]] for tracking
- Filters out irrelevant prospects automatically

### Sales Operations

#### Email Routing
Automatically routes incoming emails to appropriate channels or sales representatives, ensuring no opportunity is missed.

#### Sales Call Transcript Analyzer
Processes sales call transcripts from [[Google Sheets]]:
- Uses AI to analyze conversation content
- Generates actionable next steps
- Forwards action plans to team members via email
- Supports continuous sales improvement

#### Demo Call-Scoring
Turns meeting transcripts into lead insights by:
- Automatically spotting potential clients
- Scoring prospects based on conversation content
- Using [[Seamless AI]] for analysis

#### Sales Coach
Analyzes sales call performance:
- Retrieves call transcriptions from [[Google Drive]]
- Uses AI to determine deal status
- Provides coaching feedback to sales representatives
- Generates summaries stored in [[Google Sheets]]
- Shares insights via [[Slack]] with the sales team
